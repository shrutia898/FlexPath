Plan The Work. Work The Plan.

At FlexPath, we value the consistency and hard work that our users put into their fitness journeys so we wanted to create a place for them to visualize their short term goals by allowing them to track their weekly exercise habits.

  1. Begin with signing up for FlexPath
  2. Create your workout plan
  3. Add additional notes about your workout, meal plan, and/or other habits that you want to track
  4. Work the Plan & Track Progress!
